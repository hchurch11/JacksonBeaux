Adventure

I'm usually not one to seek new things on my own, but MOM and DAD keep me on my toes and introduce me to so many fun things! Some of my favorites so far are: Paddle Boarding, Hiking, and Jumping on to Rocks and Trees!

Paddle Boarding: I was hesitant at first, but MOM and DAD encouraged me and eventually just threw me into it, LITERALLY. They took me out on the paddle board, and I didn't know what to think at first, so my balance was not good at all.

MOM and DAD

My MOM used to hit these loud thumpers with tree branches for awhile, but now she spends most of her days staring at the thing that opens and shuts that sits on her desk. The open and shutter with the pictures on it also makes a lot of noise when she tromps her paws onto it.

Friends
